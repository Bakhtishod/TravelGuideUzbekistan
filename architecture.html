Architecture

The most famous cities with masterpieces of architectural architecture – Samarkand, Bukhara, Khiva, Shakhrisabz, Termez and Kokand attract art lovers from all over the world. Their history begins with the time of Alexander the Great and combines all the elements of different architectural styles of the ancient East. Some monuments found in various regions of Uzbekistan date back to the Stone and Bronze ages. The heyday of architectural creativity here falls on the Middle ages, when Uzbekistan was the centre of the Great Silk Road. Once having visited Uzbekistan, you will definitely want to return.
